Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 22, 2025

Welcome to NergNet! Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email, username, referral ID (via Firebase Authentication)
  • Wallet & Mining Data: Wallet address, NERG coin balance, mining activity
  • App Usage: Device model, OS, crash logs, and anonymous usage stats
  • Messages: Chat messages stored in community groups (if applicable)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate and manage user accounts
  • To track mining activity and process NERG rewards
  • To improve performance and user experience
  • To deliver notifications and updates
  • To prevent fraud and maintain a secure platform

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase services. All communications are encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS protocols. Only authorized personnel can access administrative data.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your data. We may share limited data with service providers strictly for app functionality (e.g., Firebase, Google Cloud). We comply with legal authorities when required.

5. Your Rights

  • You can access or update your personal information via the app settings.
  • You can request data deletion by emailing us at support@nergnet.app.

6. Cookies and Analytics

NergNet uses anonymous analytics to understand general usage. No personal identifiers are stored in analytics tools.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here and reflected in the app.
